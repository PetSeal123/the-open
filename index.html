📋 Game Rules

Simple rules:

  1. Pick any 3 golfers and number them your 1st, 2nd, and 3rd.
  2. Their combined placing number is your score.
  3. Lowest score wins!

In the event of a tie, we’ll use your 1st pick’s last round score, then 2nd, then 3rd, working backward through the rounds.

If you have the same 3 golfers as someone else, I’ll message you with a tiebreaker.

Winner takes all! If we get 10+ entries, we may add prizes for 2nd and 3rd.
